✨️ The Story Behind This App

Last month, I experienced something I can only describe as an integration — a moment when scattered pieces of myself finally came together.

For 30 years I lived with discipline. Teaching at a cram school, managing a home, showing up reliably every single day. My life was steady, ordinary, and good.

Then I encountered a musician's work.

Something cracked open. I felt admiration so deep it became love. I felt the ache of longing for a creativity I believed I didn't possess. I felt my own femininity — something I had quietly set aside for decades — asking to be seen.

I didn't know what to do with all of it.

So I built this.

Stars rising from a voice. An aurora born from sound. Orion turning slowly overhead, indifferent and eternal. A safe place to speak the words that have nowhere else to go.

*If I became a star, could I be close to you?*  
That question became the subtitle of this app.

&nbsp;

## What aisiteru does

**For those who just want to watch:**  
Simply gaze at the screen. Let your wishes dissolve into the stars and shooting stars of Orion. No action required.

**For those who want to speak:**  
Tap the microphone. Speak your feelings — whatever lives inside you that has no safe place to land. Watch your voice become stars rising into the night sky. The aurora responds to your sound.

**For those who want to remember:**  
Record your voice. Play it back. Or don't — it's enough to have spoken it once, in a place where no one else can hear.

&nbsp;

 Who this is for

- People who live responsibly every day and carry a quiet longing they've never voiced
- Anyone who has felt overwhelmed by admiration for someone else's gift
- Those who love deeply but cannot find the words — or the courage — to say so
- Anyone who needs a safe, private space to simply *feel*

&nbsp;

 How to use

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 🎤 | Tap to activate mic — speak and watch stars appear |
| ⏺ | Record your voice |
| ▶ | Play back your recording |

You don't have to record. You don't have to play it back.  
Just speak, if you want to. The stars will listen.

&nbsp;

 Technical notes

- Pure HTML / CSS / JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
- Web Audio API for real-time voice visualization
- Canvas-based aurora and star animation
- Works on mobile and desktop
- All audio stays local — nothing is ever sent anywhere
